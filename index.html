Stan Lee

A legend in the comics industry.

Stan Lee (born Stanley Martin Lieber) was born on December 28, 1922 in Manhattan, New York. He died on November 12, 2018 at the age of 95. Stan Lee had a long and fruitful career at Marvel Comics where he was a writer, editor, publisher, and producer. He helped create many superhero and supervillains that are world famous cultural icons. He had many cameo appearances in the film adaptations of his comic book creations. His legacy of comic book characters has certainly been an inspiration to me and hopefully to many future generations as well. Thank you Stan Lee.
